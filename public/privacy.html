Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2025

Social Fit Club ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Social Fit Club mobile and web application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account information — When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. These are stored securely via Supabase (our backend provider).

Fitness data — We store workout sessions you log, including exercise names, sets, reps, weight, and session names. This data is associated with your account and visible only to you and the community leaderboard (points and streak only — not individual workouts).

Nutrition data — Meals and macros you log are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Profile photo — If you upload a profile photo, it is stored in a secure cloud storage bucket (Supabase Storage) and displayed in the app's leaderboard.

Body log & progress photos — Body check-in entries (weight, body fat, progress photos) are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded.

Camera & video (AI features) — When you use the Meal Scan or Form Check features, images or video frames are sent to an AI processing service (Anthropic) for analysis. These are not stored after the response is returned. Use of these features is optional.

Heart rate (Bluetooth) — If you use the Health Connect feature, heart rate data is read from a paired Bluetooth device and displayed in real time. This data is not stored or transmitted.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App (authentication, syncing your workouts across devices)
  • To display the community leaderboard (points and streak only)
  • To send account-related emails (sign-up confirmation, password reset) via Resend
  • To improve the App and fix issues

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database, authentication, and file storage. Data is stored in the US. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Resend (resend.com) — transactional email delivery. Resend Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com) — AI processing for Meal Scan and Form Check features. Images sent to Anthropic are not retained after the response. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Open Food Facts (world.openfoodfacts.org) — barcode food lookups. No personal data is transmitted. Open Food Facts Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

Account data is stored on Supabase servers with row-level security — you can only read and write your own data. Passwords are never stored in plaintext; Supabase handles authentication using industry-standard practices.

Data stored locally on your device (nutrition log, body log, progress photos) remains on your device and is subject to your device's own security settings.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & correction — You can view and update your profile information at any time within the App.
  • Data deletion — To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at sfcsupport26@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.
  • Opt out of AI features — Meal Scan and Form Check are optional. Simply do not use these features if you prefer not to send data to Anthropic.

6. Children's Privacy

Social Fit Club is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
